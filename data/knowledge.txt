Anthropic is an AI safety company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and others.
Their flagship AI assistant is Claude, known for being helpful, harmless, and honest.

RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It combines a retriever with a generator.
The retriever fetches relevant documents. The generator uses those docs to write grounded answers.

LangChain is an open-source framework for building LLM applications.
It supports chains, agents, tools, memory, and vector store integrations.

ChromaDB is an open-source vector database. It stores and retrieves embeddings efficiently.
It runs locally and integrates natively with LangChain.
